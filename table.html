Community Health Toolkit The Community Health Toolkit is a global public good that includes open-source technologies for CHWs and supervisors; open-access resources; and a community of practice to advance UHC. It is the generic codebase on which the Singaporean TraceTogether contact tracing app is built. OpenTrace relies on Blue Trace, a privacy-preserving protocol for community-driven contact tracing across borders. Crowdsourcing, Screening, Self-assessment Tracking
