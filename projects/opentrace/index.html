OpenTrace

https://github.com/opentrace-community

Summary

Using Bluetooth, OpenTrace identifies other nearby phones with the app installed. It then tracks when you are in close proximity with these other persons, including timestamps. It is the generic codebase on which the Singaporean TraceTogether app is built. OpenTrace relies on Blue Trace, aprivacy-preserving protocol for community-driven contact tracing across borders.

Type of product:

Platform: Mobile App

Initial audience: Singaporean residents

Author: Government Technology Agency of Singapore

Link to live product: https://www.tracetogether.gov.sg/
